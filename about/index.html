ABOUT ME

OLIVIA KNESTAUT

As a UX/UI designer I am passionate about creativity, problem-solving, and nature. One of my main goals is to get involved with accessibility in media. I want to promote the consideration of a range of disabilities throughout every step of media creation.

Through the continuation of my UXID degree at Drexel University I hope to continue to build towards a mastery of computer science and design to help add meaningful, accessible media to the world.

Outside of my technology interests, I love to immerse myself in nature. From photographing my way across America's National Parks to taking care of houseplants I love to let Mother Nature into my life!
